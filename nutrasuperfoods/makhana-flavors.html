Makhana Flavors

raw Makhana

Our original, unsalted fox nuts that maintain their natural taste and crispiness. Perfect for those who prefer the authentic flavor or want to add their own seasoning.

Himalayan Salt & pepper

Simple, pure, and elegantly flavorful. Savor the clean and classic combination of our Himalayan Salt & Pepper makhana. We've seasoned these light and airy puffs with the pristine salt of the Himalayas and a dash of freshly ground black pepper. This flavor highlights the natural taste of the makhana, offering a perfectly balanced and sophisticated crunch.

mix Masala

A classic taste of Indian spices. Dive into the rich and authentic flavors of our Mix Masala makhana. This timeless blend is a harmonious fusion of popular Indian spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric, creating a savory and aromatic experience. It's a comforting and familiar flavor that's perfect for any time of day.

perky pudina

Crisp, cool, and refreshingly different. Experience the subtle sophistication of our Mint makhana. This unique flavor combines the light, earthy crunch of makhana with a cool, invigorating burst of natural mint. It's a surprisingly refreshing and aromatic snack perfect for a light, palate-cleansing treat. ​Mix Masala.

peri peri

​A fiery and zesty delight! Our Peri Peri makhana is for those who crave a bold kick. Infused with the iconic African bird's eye chili, this flavor delivers a spicy punch balanced with tangy notes. Each bite is a thrilling journey of heat and flavor that will awaken your taste buds.

tangy tomato

​Sweet, tangy, and irresistibly delicious. Our Tangy Tomato makhana captures the bright and vibrant taste of ripe tomatoes. With a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess, this flavor offers a delightful, mouth-watering experience reminiscent of your favorite tomato snacks. It's a fun and zesty option the whole family will love.

cream & onion

rich,creamy, and wonderfully savory. Indulge in the decadent taste of our Cream & Onion makhana. This flavor is a perfect blend of a smooth, creamy base with the sharp, savory notes of onion. It's a classic combination that delivers a satisfying and comforting crunch with every handful.
