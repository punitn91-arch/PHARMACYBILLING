About Nutra superfoods

Welcome to Nutra Superfoods, where tradition meets taste. We are proud to bring you premium nutrient rich raw and flavoured Makhana (fox nuts) directly sourced from the fertile lands of Bihar, India. Also the nutritious superfood Sattu, besan (roasted gram flour) rich in protein, fiber, and essential minerals like iron and calcium With us, quality comes first. Every product is carefully packed under strict hygienic standards to ensure freshness and purity. From nutrient-rich raw makhana or gourmet flavoured varieties, protein fiber and minerals rich Sattu, besan we create products that combine health and taste altogether.

Our mission is to provide nutrient-rich, organic products that enhance your well-being without compromising on taste.

Every product goes through rigorous quality checks to ensure you receive only the best.
